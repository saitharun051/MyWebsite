I'm Md Nazmus Sakib, a doctoral student with a focus on cybersecurity in the Department of Computer Science at The University of Texas at Dallas. My academic advisor is Professor Chung Hwan Kim. I am broadly interested in the security of systems and software. Presently, my research is centered on enhancing the security of Autonomous Cyber-Physical Systems (CPS), with a particular emphasis on autonomous vehichle security.

I completed my Bachelor's of Science in Computer Science and Engineering in 2020 at Shahjalal University of Science and Technology, Sylhet, Bangladesh. I enrolled at The University of Texas at Dallas in August 2023 and commenced my doctoral studies.

Research Interests

  • Software Security
